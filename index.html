WEEKLY ADJUSTMENT STRATEGY
How to track progress and what to do when gains stall
📏 How to Track Progress
Weigh daily — same time, post-toilet, before food. Record in Weight Log tab.
Weekly avg — add Mon–Sun weights, divide by 7. This is your real number. Ignore daily spikes.
Progress photos — front/side/back every Sunday morning in same lighting.
Strength log — track weights lifted in gym. Progressive overload = muscle signal.
Tape measure — chest, arm, waist every 2 weeks.
🚦 Decision Framework
✅ On Track: +0.3–0.6 kg/week avg
Keep everything the same. Don't change a working plan.
⚠️ Slow: +0.1–0.2 kg/week
Add 150–200 kcal/day (extra shake, extra rice portion). Recheck consistency of meals.
🔴 Stalled: No change / loss
Add 300 kcal/day. Check if actually eating consistently. Add a full shake daily. Verify sleep (8hr).
